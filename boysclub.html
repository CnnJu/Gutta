GUTTEKLUBBEN (BOYS CLUB)

Gutteklubben Goes To Hell (teaser) from Gutta on Vimeo.

Gutteklubben Goes To Hell was Gutta's contribution to the 50 years anniversary of the decriminalization of the penal code's paragraph number 213; the ban on sex between men in Norway (also known as Queer Culture Year) in 2022. Here we chose to dive into the very pivot point of paragraph 213; the butthole.
Gutta created a delicious sinful hell, where the audience could come in contact with their innermost desires, and especially their anus. Gutta invited them to get to know sides of themselves and preferences they didn't even know they had. In short, Gutta gave the audience everything they didn't know they wanted.
