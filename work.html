Pride

Pride + Gutta = Eternal Magical Love

Gutta loves all the queers and is ecstatic to celebrate love. There's a particular letter in LGBTQIA+ that they have a big heart for, namely Lesbians.
By coming out as Lesbros, Gutta finally found their rightful place in the queer struggle. Gutta aren't just making Pride shows and happenings; they're opening all your channels—intellectual, emotional, aesthetic, and sexual.
They invite the audience into a deep, soft, warm, and wet state where you can connect with your innermost desires and discover new sides of yourselves. Together, we'll expand our consciousness, dissolving the boundaries between us. Come and be opened up in our safe embrace, and let's celebrate together.
Gutta gives you everything you didn't know you wanted. They are your new sexuality.
For many years Gutta has tailored shows, happenings, performances, talks and parades for queer contexts. We have been part of Prides in Oslo, Stavanger, Lidköping, Bodø, Træna, Drammen and Trondheim.

GUTTEKLUBBEN (BOYS CLUB)

Gutteklubben Goes To Hell was Gutta's contribution to the 50 years anniversary of the decriminalization of the penal code's paragraph number 213; the ban on sex between men in Norway (also known as Queer Culture Year) in 2022. Here we chose to dive into the very pivot point of paragraph 213; the butthole.
Gutta created a delicious sinful hell, where the audience could come in contact with their innermost desires, and especially their anus. Gutta invited them to get to know sides of themselves and preferences they didn't even know they had. In short, Gutta gave the audience everything they didn't know they wanted.

CULT MOVIE

Gutta presents: GOD (2020)

In times of crises the people need strong leaders to look up to. A great patriarch, a stable father figure. Furthermore, we Gutta need the people to gather around us as we go in and out of quarantine, we miss being embraced. Larry, Robin, Lavrans and RichHard seek patriarchal inspiration by calling on the ancestor himself; God.

Movie stars

The members of Gutta can be seen in several movies.

Like a Lady
featuring: Larry, Robin, RichHard and Lavrans
Gutta has made their first appearance as movie stars in the music video for the band Pom Poko's song "Like a Lady," directed by Marin Håskjold, which was nominated for “Kortfilmfestivalen 2021” (the National Norwegian short film festival). Link here
UTESKOLEN / The Outdoor School
featuring: Larry and RichHard
In this new movie directed by Mariken Halle (premiering in 2025), we are following kids and adults during one year of the pandemic, who are struggling with their relationship to each other and to nature. Larry and RichHard are environmental entrepreneurs with healing skills.
Snake Oil
featuring: Robin and Lavrans
Rose Hammers new movie “Snake Oil” (premiering in 2025) is about Norwegian oil history. Robin and Lavrans are playing father and son - both two greedy rich business men from Texas.
