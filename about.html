Gutta is a drag king collective based in Oslo consisting of the four performing artists Desiree Bøgh Vaksdal (NO), Josephine Kylén Collins (SE), Lærke Grøntved (DK) and Ann-Christin Kongsness (NO). We explore our own masculinity in its complexities and nuances, at the same time as we shamelessly sample and shake male stereotypes. The four friends Larry, RichHard, Lavrans and Robin were born on Valentine's day in 2019, in front of a hungry and eager audience.

“When Larry sings the world echoes.”- Critic
“Lavrans, like Jesus, turns water into wine.” - A theater director
“RichHard has many tricks up his sleeve. He got the audience to follow his orders as dogs on stage” - Journalist
“Freedom is to see Robin dance his essence until it belongs to the collective” - An admirer, friend and politician
