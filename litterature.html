My Dick Has No Eyes
For many years it has been this way
You could put yourself in another person's place
In art especially
I can put myself in someone else's place
In a woman's place
Through art I can describe her from the inside
That's how it is
People talk so much about “male gaze” but
Let's not focus so much on whether one has a penis or not
I don't think we´re getting anywhere
Would that now change which eyes I see with?
I see with human eyes
Because strangely enough …
My Dick has no eyes
